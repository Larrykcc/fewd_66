Blueberry Smoothie!

  • Click For Homepage

It's that time again...get ready to kick back with a nice refreshing Blueberry Smoothie!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup blueberries (frozen or fresh)
  • 1 (8 ounce) container plain yogurt
  • 3/4 cup 2% reduced-fat milk
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Duration

Prep: 5min
Ready: 5min

Instructions

Blend the blueberries, yogurt, milk, sugar, vanilla, and nutmeg in a blender until frothy, scraping down the sides of the blender with a spatula occasionally. Serve. Enjoy solo or with a loved one!
